About Just Animate

Just Animate makes it easy to animate websites. It comes with over 70 animations for entrances, exits, and of course fun. The main goal of Just Animate is to provide a simple way to get started with animations built upon the Web Animations API and to grow from there to building animation sequences and more complex timelines.
